Biography Of Dr. Jeetendra Patel

Dr. Jeetendra Patel doesn't need any introduction. He is a prominent astrologer, aura scientist, and spiritual healer from Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. He has been working in the industry for almost 20 years after realising at age 10 that he had a God-given knack for it. He had a firm grasp on his knowledge by the time he was 19 and offered his assistance to the public for the first time.

Dr. Patel has a Master's degree in Chemistry, which may surprise some people. Following the call of his heart, he went on to earn a doctorate in astrology and aura science. His father likewise has a strong scientific background and is well respected in the scientific community thanks to his work at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). Indeed, even his brother is a scientist.
