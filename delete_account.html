How to delete a JP Astrologer Account?

Permanently delete your account, including all data, settings, and history. Here are the steps below:

  1. Open the app.
  2. If not logged in, login to the app using your credentials.
  3. Navigate to the "Profile" tab.
  4. Select the option to delete your account.
  5. Confirm that you understand your data will be deleted permanently.
  6. Click on "Delete Account" or a similar confirmation button.
  7. Receive confirmation that your account has been successfully deleted.
